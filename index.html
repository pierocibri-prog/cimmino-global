The stuff nobody warns you about

You have a great product idea. You find suppliers online. And then reality hits, because sourcing from China is nothing like it looks from the outside.

01
You can't tell who's actually a factory
Most suppliers on public platforms are traders pretending to be manufacturers. They look the same, say the same things, and you have no way to know until the wrong shipment arrives at your door.
02
You're paying more than you should
Every middleman between you and the factory adds a margin. Two or three layers in and you're paying well above the real factory price, invisibly, every single order.
03
The details that kill your margins
Missing a certification? Your product gets blocked at customs. Wrong specs? You eat the cost. These aren't edge cases, they happen to almost everyone doing this for the first time.
04
Communication is harder than you think
Time zones, language barriers, early morning calls, and even then you're never quite sure if what was agreed is actually what's being made. That uncertainty is exhausting, and costly.
05
Minimum orders that don't match your reality
Factories want volume. You want to test first. That gap is where a lot of good ideas die, or where people over-commit and end up sitting on inventory they can't move.

FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot.

How much does it cost?
Every project is different, so we don't publish a fixed price. What we can tell you is that we work with a project fee agreed upfront and a success fee only if you decide to buy. No surprises, no hidden charges. We talk about it in the first call.
How long does the process take?
From the first conversation to a verified manufacturer report, typically 2 to 4 weeks. It depends on the complexity of the product and how quickly we can validate the right candidates. We move fast, but we don't cut corners.
What categories do you work with?
Almost anything that can be manufactured: electronics, apparel, accessories, food products, pharma packaging, hardware, toys, furniture, consumer goods. If you're not sure whether your product fits, just ask. The intake takes 5 minutes and we'll tell you honestly.
How do I know the manufacturer is real?
That's exactly the right question. We access manufacturers through our institutional Alibaba agreement, which means they're audited and verified before we even contact them. We don't search the public platform everyone else uses. That's the whole point.
Do I need to have everything figured out before reaching out?
Not at all. Some clients come with full specs and samples. Others come with just an idea. We ask the right questions to figure out where you are and what you need. The earlier you involve us, the more we can help you avoid expensive mistakes.
